# [GCD](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/gcd/1?page=4&category=Java&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Given two numbers&nbsp;A and B. The task is to find the GCD of&nbsp; A and B.The GCD of two numbers is the largest number that can divide both A and B perfectly.
Examples:  
Input: A = 6, B = 9
Output: 3
Explanation: After 3 there is no number that can divide both 6 and 9 perfectly.
Input: A = 10, B = 15
Output: 5
Explanation: 5 is the greatest common divisor of 10 and 15.
Constraints:1&nbsp; ≤&nbsp; A, B&nbsp; ≤&nbsp; 100

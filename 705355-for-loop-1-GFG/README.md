# [For Loop - 1](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/for-loop-1-1605617969/1?page=6&category=Java&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
You are given a number n, you need to print its multiplication table in a single line.
Examples:
Input: n = 5
Output: 5 10 15 20 25 30 35 40 45 50
Input: n = 6
Output: 6 12 18 24 30 36 42 48 54 60

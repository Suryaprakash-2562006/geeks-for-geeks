# [Right Angle Triangle 2](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/right-angle-triangle-2-1605689820/1?page=4&category=Java&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Given an integer s.&nbsp;Write a program to print the Right angle triangle.&nbsp;The length&nbsp;of the perpendicular and base&nbsp;is s.&nbsp;&nbsp;Note: There is exactly single space ' ' after a start '*'. Print a new line after printing the Triangle.
Example 1:  
Input: s = 9
Output:** **   **     **       **         **           **             ** * * * * * * * * 
Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 9.
Input: s = 4Output:** **   ** * * *Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 4.
Constraints:1 ≤ s ≤ 12

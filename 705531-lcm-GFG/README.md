# [LCM](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/lcm/1?page=4&category=Java&difficulty=Basic&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Given two numbers&nbsp;a&nbsp;and&nbsp;b. The task is to find out their LCM.
Examples:
Input: a = 5, b = 10
Output: 10
Explanation: LCM of 5 and 10 is 10
Input: a = 14, b = 8
Output: 56
Explanation: LCM of 14 and 8 is 56
Constraints:1&nbsp; &lt;=&nbsp; a , b&nbsp; &lt;=&nbsp; 103

## Easy
Given an integer n.&nbsp;Write a program to print the Right angle triangle.&nbsp;The length&nbsp;of the perpendicular and base&nbsp;is n.&nbsp;&nbsp;
Examples :  
Input: n = 9
Output:** **   **     **       **         **           **             ** * * * * * * * * 
Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 9.
Input: n = 4Output:** **   ** * * *Explanation: Length of perpendicular and base of triangle is 4.
Constraints:1 ≤ n ≤ 12

# [GCD](https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/gcd--144435/1?page=4&category=Java&difficulty=Basic&sortBy=submissions)
## Easy
Given two numbers&nbsp;a&nbsp;and&nbsp;b. The task is to find the GCD of&nbsp;&nbsp;a&nbsp;and&nbsp;b.The GCD of two numbers is the largest number that can divide both a and b perfectly.
Examples:
Input: a = 6, b = 9
Output: 3
Explanation: After 3 there is no number that can divide both 6 and 9 perfectly.
Input: a = 10, b = 15
Output: 5
Explanation: 5 is the greatest common divisor of 10 and 15.
Constraints:1&nbsp; ≤&nbsp; a, b&nbsp; ≤&nbsp; 100
